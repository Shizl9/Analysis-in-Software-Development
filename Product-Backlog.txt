Product Backlog Development 

Definition: 
A product backlog is an ordered list of all features, enhancements, fixes, and technical work required in a product. It serves as a central repository of work items for the development team. 

Creating & Structuring a Product Backlog: 

Break down features into small, manageable user stories 

Assign unique IDs or labels to each story 

Include descriptions, priorities, and initial estimates
